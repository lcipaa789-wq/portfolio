# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters and HR professionals scanning developer candidates. They spend seconds on a page: they need to see role, projects, and personality fast. They are not necessarily technical, but they recognize polish and confidence.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio for Tsydyp Lundukov, a Frontend Developer. Exists to convert a recruiter visit into a conversation. Success looks like: they remember the name, click through to projects, and reach out.

## Brand Personality

Precise. Electric. Deliberate.

## Anti-references

- Bootstrap-style template portfolios: uniform card grids, generic nav, blue-gradient hero
- Terminal clichés: matrix green on black, monospace everywhere, scan-line effects
- Overloaded glassmorphism: blurs and frosted panels used decoratively throughout

## Design Principles

1. Typography earns the first impression. The name is the loudest thing on the page.
2. Precision over decoration. Every element has a reason; nothing is there for "tech feeling."
3. Recruiter-legible. The role, projects, and contact path are findable in under 10 seconds.
4. Consistent visual language across both pages. Same photo treatment, same type system, same palette.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion support required for all entrance animations.
